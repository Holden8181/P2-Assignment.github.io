Crafting and Smelting

As you may have guessed, crafting is an essential part of the game, Minecraft. Crafting allows you to create better materials with what you have. The first step in all of this? Getting a crafting table(as said above). Minecraft being the innovative game it is, there are many things you can do that aren't pertinent to your survival with a crafting table and the right materials, such as making a roller coaster, fireworks, or even tapestries to decorate your house with. Of course there are a few essential items you will need to gather first, such as wooden tools to eventually be upgraded, and transforming wood from trees into regular wooden blocks.
